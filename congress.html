Both in the federal and state government, thelegislative branch is responsible for makingand passing laws. The federal legislativebranch, Congress, operates based on theConstitution, which states in Article 1 whatareas of law are meant for this Congress,such as issues that affect the entirecountry, including declaration of war andregulation of the military. Meanwhile, statelegislation operates based on the specificconstitution of that state.
